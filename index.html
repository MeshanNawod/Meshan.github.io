Battle of Freshers 2023

Our purpose is to...

To connect students who are passionate about drama and theatre.

To create a network of actors, directors, playwrights, and drama enthusiasts.

To enhance interest, understanding, and research in the field of drama and theatre.

To disseminate knowledge about drama and theatre among its members and the wider community.

To develop leadership, teamwork, and other soft skills through drama activities.

To organize lectures, seminars, workshops, and other related activities for members and other interested parties to introduce contemporary drama.

To publish magazines and articles that promote interest in drama and theatre.

To promote and assist the exchange of information related to drama and theatre among its members and other interested individuals via the website.
